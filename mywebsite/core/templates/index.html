    About Red Letter Basics

    Many Christians struggle to read and understand the Bible, even those who have been in the church for years. Research shows that while most believers acknowledge the Bible as God’s Word, they often feel unequipped to study it effectively. Without a clear understanding of Scripture, spiritual growth stalls, and many drift away from their relationship with Jesus.

    At Red Letter Basics, we believe the solution isn’t just more reading plans or resources—it’s teaching people how to engage with Scripture in a way that brings clarity and confidence.

    Our approach is simple: start with the words of Jesus. Just as someone learning to cook doesn’t need to attend culinary school, understanding the Bible doesn’t require a seminary degree. With the right foundation, anyone can grow in their faith and grasp the truth of God’s Word.

    Our Mission: To help people know and listen to Jesus by equipping them with tools that make the Bible understandable and transformative.

    Our Vision: A world where every believer can open the Bible, read the words of Jesus, and understand His truth with confidence.

    Whether you're an individual, a family, or a church, Red Letter Basics is here to help you grow in your faith and deepen your relationship with Jesus.
